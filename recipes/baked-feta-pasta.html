Baked Feta Pasta

Description

Baked feta pasta is a simple recipe made by oven roasting a block of feta cheese with cherry tomatoes to create a salty and cheesy sauce!

You don't need to change the oven temperature. The OG recipe calls for baking the feta with the tomatoes at 400Â° for 30 minutes then kicking up for the last 10 minutes. After 30 minutes in the oven, I found my feta well on its way to a pretty shade of golden on top. To avoid burning any extra additions like garlic and herbs, I think it's best to keep the temperature steady. While many recipes only call for one pint of tomatoes, after testing this recipe, we decided to add two for a fuller, more flavorful sauce. A scoop of pasta water will help stretch your sauce to coat your pasta more smoothly and generously.
